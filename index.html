01 · Philosophy

A personal site should feel like a scene, not just a layout.

This redesign moves away from pure restraint and toward something more atmospheric. The goal is still elegance, but with visible weather, emotional color, and the kind of visual depth that feels closer to music and memory than to a standard portfolio template.

Design intention
“

Make the page feel suspended between writing, weather, and the afterglow of sound.

The first version was clean, but too dry. This version keeps discipline while adding atmosphere: layered light, dusk colors, translucent panels, and a quieter kind of emotional richness. It should feel composed, but no longer plain.

The influence here is not about showing specific taste directly. It is about translating a love of luminous skies, melancholy distance, piano-like softness, and cinematic stillness into the grammar of the page.

What remains Personal writing, technology, life, and a human voice that does not pretend to be a brand.
What changed The site now has color temperature, atmospheric depth, and a stronger emotional first impression.
What it should feel like A place where technical thought and private weather can coexist without explanation.

About me

Dongyuan

I’m interested in technology not only as a technical system, but as something that shapes attention, relationships, work, and identity. This site is where technical notes and personal reflections can sit next to each other without apology.
